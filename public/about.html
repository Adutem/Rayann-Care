Meet Dr. Cindy Sonnier

Dr. Cindy Sonnier is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW) and the founder of RayAnn Care Psychological Services. Holding a Doctor of Social Work (DSW) degree and licenses in Florida, Texas, North Carolina, and Alabama, Dr. Sonnier brings over a decade of experience in delivering psychological evaluations, brief therapy, and professional coaching.

Her work is grounded in cultural humility, clinical integrity, and a deep belief in the power of accessible, affirming mental health care.

"I created RayAnn Care to offer accessible, ethical, and empowering mental health services to diverse communities across the U.S. As a veteran and mental health provider, I understand the importance of creating spaces where people feel seen, safe, and supported."

Dr. Sonnier’s approach blends clarity, compassion, and clinical excellence. Whether you’re navigating trauma, seeking immigration support, or building your private practice, she ensures every interaction is personalized, empathetic, and focused on your growth and healing.
