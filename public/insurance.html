Payment & Insurance

At RayAnn Care Psychological Services, we are committed to providing high-quality, accessible care. Below is important information regarding payment options, insurance, and financing:

Private Pay Services

We are a private pay practice and do not accept insurance at this time. This allows us to provide you with the utmost confidentiality, flexibility, and personalized care without the limitations of insurance networks.

Current Service Fees:

  • Immigration Evaluations – $1,000 (completed within 7 business days)

  • Mini EMDR Intensives – $1,875 (3-day package)

  • Group Clinical Supervision (Social Work Students) – $50 per session

Payment Methods Accepted

We accept the following payment methods:

  • All major credit/debit cards

  • HSA/FSA cards (check with your plan)

  • Zelle or ACH bank transfer

  • Secure payments through our client portal (via SimplePractice)

Flexible Payment Options

To help make services more accessible, we offer financing through trusted partners:

  • CareCredit – Healthcare financing with 0% interest promotional plans (for eligible clients)

You will see these options during checkout or can request financing details prior to your appointment.

Superbills for Reimbursement

Although we do not bill insurance directly, we can provide a superbill upon request for eligible services. You may submit this to your insurance provider for potential out-of-network reimbursement. Please check with your insurance carrier for details.

Cancellations & Late Payments

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or no-shows may be charged the full session fee. Late payments may incur additional administrative fees.

Have Questions?

We are happy to assist you with any questions about pricing, financing, or superbill requests. Please reach out to us at info@rayanncare.com or 1-888-310-6692.
