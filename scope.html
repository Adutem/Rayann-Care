Scope of Services

RayAnn Care Psychological Services offers psychological evaluations, mini EMDR intensives, coaching, and clinical supervision. These services are designed to support mental clarity, goal achievement, and healing. We do not provide ongoing psychotherapy unless otherwise arranged. Coaching is not a substitute for therapy or medical care. Coaching clients must understand that no diagnosis or treatment is provided in coaching sessions.

Licensure & Service Areas

Dr. Cindy Sonnier, DSW, LCSW, is licensed to provide clinical services in

  •  Florida

  • Texas

  • North Carolina

  • Alabama

Psychological evaluations and EMDR intensives are only available to clients residing in these states. Coaching services may be available nationwide depending on state regulations.

Privacy & Confidentiality

All sessions are conducted using secure, HIPAA-compliant platforms. We follow all federal and state confidentiality laws. Client information is only disclosed with written consent or as required by law (e.g., risk of harm to self or others, child abuse, court order). For more information, please refer to your Informed Consent form prior to your first session.

Crisis & Emergency Disclaimer

RayAnn Care Psychological Services does not provide crisis intervention or emergency services. If you are in crisis or need immediate support, call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room. You may also contact the 24/7 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline at 988.

Technology Limitations

Please note that telehealth services depend on a reliable internet connection and privacy on the client’s end. Clients are responsible for ensuring they are in a quiet, confidential space for virtual sessions.
